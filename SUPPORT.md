## Before opening an issue

1. **Search existing issues and discussions** to avoid duplicates.
2. **Confirm against the latest minor version** — we ship fixes on the latest line only.
3. **Build a minimal reproduction** for bugs. Forking one of the [examples/](https://github.com/level0x40/virtual-frame/tree/main/examples) is the fastest path.

The issue templates will guide you through the rest.

## Response expectations

Virtual Frame is maintained by Level 0x40 Labs. Maintainer time is finite and triage is best-effort. We aim to respond to:

- **Bug reports with a clear reproduction** within a few business days.
- **Security reports** per the SLAs in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
- **Feature requests** during regular roadmap cycles — expect a longer turnaround, especially for proposals without a contributor attached.
- **Discussions** as time permits; community members are encouraged to chime in.

If a thread has gone quiet and you have new information (additional repro, workaround, related context), reply on the same thread rather than opening a new one.
